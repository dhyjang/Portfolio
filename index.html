Module 05 — About

From the classroom to the design studio

I spent six years teaching math in DC Public Schools before moving into instructional design — first at Johns Hopkins Carey Business School and the University of Maryland School of Dentistry, and now in community college online learning. That classroom experience is the foundation of everything I design: I've been on the other side of the materials, and I know what clarity, structure, and access mean to a learner.

Today my work sits at the intersection of learning science, accessibility, and technology. Whether I'm reviewing a course against quality standards, building an automation that gives faculty their time back, or helping an institution think through responsible AI adoption, my goal is the same: learning experiences that work for everyone.
